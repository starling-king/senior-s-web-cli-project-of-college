Introduction to Senior Citizen Rights

Senior citizens are the backbone of our society. They have contributed to the development of the nation through their hard work and experience. However, with advancing age, they often face physical, financial, and emotional challenges. Recognizing these difficulties, the Government of India has formulated various laws, schemes, and benefits to ensure their well-being and protect their rights.

These rights are not just privileges but essential provisions to guarantee a life of dignity, respect, and security. It includes the right to financial assistance, affordable healthcare, safe travel, and protection against abuse. By understanding these rights, senior citizens can lead an independent and empowered life.
