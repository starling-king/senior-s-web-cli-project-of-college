Health & Medical Benefits

Ensuring accessible, affordable, and respectful healthcare for elderly citizens.

Ayushman Bharat (AB-PMJAY) Expansion

Healthcare expenses are a major concern for the elderly. To address this, the Government of India has significantly expanded the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.

This landmark expansion covers all senior citizens aged 70 years and above, completely irrespective of their income status. It provides free health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.

Note: Seniors already covered under other public health schemes (like CGHS or ECHS) can choose to either stay with their current scheme or switch to Ayushman Bharat, but they cannot avail benefits from both simultaneously.

National Programme for the Health Care of the Elderly (NPHCE)

Recognizing the unique medical needs of the aging population, the NPHCE was established to provide dedicated, specialized care for senior citizens across government medical facilities.

  • Dedicated Queues: Separate registration and pharmacy queues for seniors to reduce waiting times.
  • Geriatric Clinics: Specialized outpatient departments (OPDs) focusing on age-related ailments.
  • Reserved Beds: Dedicated wards and beds in district hospitals to ensure immediate admission and care.
