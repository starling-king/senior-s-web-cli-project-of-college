Economic Rights & Old Age Pension

Ensuring financial security and independence for senior citizens.

Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS)

Financial insecurity is one of the major challenges faced by elderly citizens. The Government of India established IGNOAPS under the National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) to provide a social safety net.

This scheme provides a monthly pension directly to the bank accounts of eligible senior citizens, ensuring they have basic financial support to live with dignity and independence.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The applicant must be 60 years or older.
  • The applicant must belong to a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household according to the criteria prescribed by the Government of India.
  • The applicant must not be receiving financial support from family members or any other government pension scheme.

Required Documents

To apply for the pension, please ensure you have clear copies of the following documents:

  • Aadhaar Card (For age and identity verification)
  • BPL Ration Card (Proof of income status)
  • Active Bank Account Passbook (For direct fund transfer)
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs
  • Address Proof (Voter ID, Electricity Bill, etc.)
